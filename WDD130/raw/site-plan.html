Overview

Purpose

The course will use a fictitious hotel company website project to be the learning project in which principles can be applied.
Each week, additional components, content, technologies, and development practices will be added to the same project.

Typography

Heading Font: Vidaloka, serif

The stroke helps letter to stand out, specially for titles and headings/subheadings

Paragraph Font: Lato, sans-serif

We use sans serifs for paragraphs and texts because normally uses a small font type that is easy to read

Normal paragraph example

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world.

Colored paragraph example

The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.
