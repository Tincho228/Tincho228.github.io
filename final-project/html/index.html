Temple Open Houses and Dedications

Visitors of all ages are invited to a free public open house held for most new or renovated temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The open house includes a video and a walk-through tour of the temple. If you would like to view the introductory video ahead of time, please click on the video to the left. After the temple is formally dedicated, entrance is reserved for faithful members of the Church.
